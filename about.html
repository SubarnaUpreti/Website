What is Conservation Photography?


The Story of Good Photographers

The Good Photographers is a highly effective wildlife conservation photography charity funding key conservation projects across Asia and Nepal. Our mission is to increase awareness around the world about the need to protect and conserve wildlife and their habitats.

Our ground-based conservation partners are dedicated and professional organisations with a deep understanding of the landscapes in which they operate and our relationships are steeped in mutual respect and admiration, nurtured over many years. More than ever before, we face daily news of the devastation that humans inflict on wildlife and on our planet; news of vanishing species and wild spaces. Good Photographers invests in economically and socially vulnerable communities with programmes that have a real impact on real people in real places from the fund they raise by selling thier wildlife prints.



Our Origin

The Good Photographers takes a holistic approach to conservation, recognising the essential role that communities and education both play in ensuring conservation successes. We also recognise the need to tackle environmental and wildlife crime from grassroots to the world stage across all forums and platforms available for change.

Our online store has become synonymous with quality, and we ensure a continuous variety of fantastic wildlife prints that will help you to decorate your wall and protect the animals in need. Check it out and start conserving today.


Want to know more?



For each and every one of our wildlife art prints, we have committed to donating 10% of each purchase to wildlife conservation organizations in need—thus reinforcing their desire to give back to the beautiful animals they so lovingly document.



Through our stunning photography, we aim to inspire others regarding the beauty, splendor, and delicacy of nature. Good Photographers ethos—Sacred Nature—reminds us of the fragile nature of our planet and the importance of connecting back to nature.



In addition to our artistry, Good Photographers have also committed their lives to fighting for wildlife conservation. They are Patrons and Ambassadors for a number of conservation organizations including the Cheetah Conservation Fund in Chitwon, the Bardia Wildlife Trust’s Cheetah and Tiger Projects, Rhino Ark, Bird Conservation Nepal, the Red Panda Conservation Trust and Trust for tigers.

Good Photographers is a company based on buying and selling wildlife prints that helps in the conservation of wildlife.
